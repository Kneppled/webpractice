Facts

  • Youtube is the second most viewed website, behind google.

  • YouTube reaches more adults age 18-34 than any cable network.

  • 80% of YouTube traffic comes from outside the US.

  • More content is uploaded to YouTube in 60 days than the three major American television networks created in the last 60 years.

  • Forbes recently valued YouTube at about $70 billion dollars.

  • 1,000 new videos are uploaded by the time one finishes watching one video on YouTube. However, if you negate that fact, it would take, it would take you 60,000 years of non-stop watching to watch each and every video on YouTube.

  • The three YouTube founders: Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, and Jawed Karim worked for PayPal. They used their money from eBay when they buyout of PayPal to fund youtube.

  • YouTube was not in high definition until the global rollout in November 2009.

  • More than 20 percent of videos are switched off within the first 10 seconds of playback.

  • YouTube is the second largest search engine in the world.
