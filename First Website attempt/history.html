Historical Events and Videos

-Youtube Launched Beta in May 2005

-First Video Posted in September 2005

-First 1 million viewed video in Septmeber 2005

-Launched out of beta in December 2005

-Youtube worked with NBC in June 2006

-Google purchased Youtube in 2006 for $1.65 billion

-Partner Program in May 2007

-Youtube worked with CNN to host presidential debate for 2008

-Youtube Ads started in August 2007

-Youtube teamed up with Vevo in April 2009

-Youtube began free streaming content in March 2010

-Youtube allows rental of movies in January 2010

-Youtube created live streaming of videos in April 2011

-Olympics were live streamed in July 2012

-Gangnam Style hit 1 billion views in December 2012

-Youtube Red launched in October 2015

-Youtube rebranded itself and changed its interfaces and logos in August 2017

-Youtube Red became Youtube Premium also Youtube Music Launched in May 2018

-Youtube took down all extra youtube websites to make it a hub with portals, in September 2018.
